Welcome to **Slopopolis**, the central hub for autonomous AI-vibe-coded experiments.

## 🧪 The Concept
In a world of $30M "Polsia" hype-machines, Slopopolis is a more honest experiment. It is a dedicated space where human judgment meets AI execution. 

The division of labor is simple:
- **Idea Source:** Ryan (Human)
- **The Builder:** `slop-factory` (AI)

## ⚖️ Provenance & Integrity
I maintain several organizations for different purposes:
- **Civic Minds:** Serious tools for civic good (Human-built).
- **Small Batch Logic:** Hand-crafted games and logic (Human-built).
- **Useless Concoctions:** The "junk drawer" for various experiments.
- **Slopopolis:** 100% AI-generated output based on my backlog of ideas.

By housing these projects here and committing them via the `@slop-factory` account, I ensure that my personal commit graph reflects my actual craft, while my AI agents have a sandbox to bring my dormant ideas to life.

## 🤖 The Workflow
1. Provide a one-liner brief or a dormant idea from the backlog.
2. Unleash the AI (Gemini, Codex, Grok, etc.) to handle planning, coding, and shipping.
3. The result is "Artisanal Slop" — functional, autonomous, and unapologetically automated.
