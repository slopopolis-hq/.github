# Slopfesto

Slopopolis is what happens when you give a robot a list of half-baked ideas and tell it to "figure it out." We specialize in high-velocity, low-utility digital slop, because shipping a joke is more fun than having a career.

The split is honest: human ideas meet machine hands. Every spark comes from [@ryanphanna](https://github.com/ryanphanna), and every commit belongs to [@slop-factory](https://github.com/slop-factory). We operate at infinite scale—zero employees, zero revenue, and zero clue what we're doing.
